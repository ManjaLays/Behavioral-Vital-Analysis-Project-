Behavioral & Vital Analysis of Immobilized Patients

A Smart Healthcare Monitoring System

Project Overview

This project focuses on the development of a smart system that monitors both physical and mental health aspects of immobilized patients. Using vital signs like heart rate, body temperature, and behavioral indicators such as sleep patterns and emotional state, the system provides continuous observation of patient well-being.


Key Features

  • Real-time data collection from wearable sensors and monitoring devices.
  • Analysis of behavioral patterns and vital signs using machine learning models.
  • Prediction of potential health risks and automatic alert generation for healthcare providers.
  • User-friendly dashboard for tracking patient status and alerts.

Impact

This system enhances patient care by providing early warnings, improving response times, and enabling more informed decision-making by medical professionals. It particularly benefits patients who are unable to communicate their needs due to immobilization.
